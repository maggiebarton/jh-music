03 / Media & Bio

Serve the song.

Joshua T. Hester is a Wisconsin-based Americana and folk singer-songwriter whose music is rooted in storytelling, lived experience, and the traditions of American roots music.

A road-tested multi-instrumentalist and former member of Listening Party, Hester blends folk, country, Americana, and roots influences into character-driven songs with a distinctly Midwestern perspective.
